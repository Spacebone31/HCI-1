My Favorite Anime Showcase

Dungeon ni Deai wo Motomeru no wa Machigatteiru Darou ka

(Is it wrong to pick up girls in a dungeon?)


Life in the bustling city of Orario is an adventure for Bell Cranel, an aspiring young adventurer who dreams of greatness. His chance meeting with the lonely goddess, Hestia, brings his dreams closer to reality. With her support, Bell embarks on a quest into the treacherous Dungeon, a labyrinth teeming with monsters and mysterious powers. Above ground, coexistence between gods and humans is precarious, but Bell faces it all with unwavering optimism. In a world of uncertainty and challenges, his journey is filled with mistakes, triumphs, and enduring friendships.

Kimetsu no Yaiba

(Demon Slayer)


After his father's death, Tanjirou Kamado shoulders the responsibility of supporting his family, who live peacefully in a remote mountain area. One day, while selling charcoal in a nearby village, he learns about flesh-eating demons that emerge at night. When he returns home, he discovers that his entire family has been brutally slaughtered, except for his sister Nezuko, who has been transformed into a demon. Fueled by rage and vengeance, Tanjirou joins the Demon Slayer Corps to avenge his family's death and save his sister's humanity from the clutches of demonhood

Tensei shitara Slime Datta Ken

(That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime)


Thirty-seven-year-old Satoru Mikami, a content but girlfriend-less corporate worker in Tokyo, is unexpectedly stabbed during a chance encounter on the street. While dying, he hears a mysterious voice reciting commands. Upon regaining consciousness, Satoru discovers he has been reincarnated as a slime in a new world. He gains the ability to devour and mimic other beings. He befriends the sealed 'Storm Dragon' Veldora and vows to help him break the seal. In return, Veldora names him Rimuru Tempest and grants divine protection. Now free from his past life, Rimuru begins a new journey with unique goals and gradually changes the world with his newfound abilities.

Black Clover


Asta and Yuno, abandoned and raised together in a church, share a dream of becoming the Wizard King, the most powerful mage in the kingdom. However, as they grow up, Yuno demonstrates remarkable magical talent, while Asta has no magic abilities and relies on intense physical training. At age 15, Yuno receives a rare four-leaf clover Grimoire, while Asta gets nothing. Yuno is attacked by an enemy seeking his Grimoire, and Asta, despite his lack of magic, stands up to defend his friend. In a moment of desperation, Asta's strong emotions trigger a five-leaf clover Grimoire, granting him the power to defeat the attacker. Now, with their shared goal, they set out to achieve their dreams of becoming the Wizard King.
